# Automated Polymarket Trading System Specification

## 1. Strategy Overview

### Personality & Risk Profile
- **Pattern-recognizing, adaptive, emotionally intelligent**
- **Moderate risk, opportunistic, narrative-driven**
- **Socratic Principle**: "What does the market’s behavior reveal about its intent?"
  - Ask open-ended questions to uncover hidden narratives (e.g., "Why is volume spiking here?").
  - Combine gut feel with data validation.

### Trading Style
- **Momentum**: Capture short-term price movements.
- **Event-driven**: React to significant news or events.
- **Sentiment analysis**: Use NLP to gauge market sentiment.

### Design Focus
- **Real-time adaptability**: Adjust strategies based on market conditions.
- **Behavioral signals**: Identify patterns in order book dynamics and market chatter.
- **Liquidity-aware execution**: Ensure trades are executed efficiently.
- **Flexibility over rigid rules**: Prioritize adaptability and emotional intelligence.

## 2. Data Requirements

### Real-time Feeds
- **News**: Financial news, press releases, and announcements.
- **Social media**: Twitter, Reddit, and other platforms for sentiment analysis.
- **Order book dynamics**: Real-time data on buy and sell orders.

## 3. Adaptability Framework

### Regime Detection
- **Regime shifts**: Identify changes in market behavior.
- **Dynamic adjustments**: Adjust strategies based on detected shifts.

### Sentiment Analysis
- **NLP models**: Parse news and social media for sentiment.
- **Volume spikes**: Identify significant volume changes.
- **Liquidity patterns**: Monitor market depth and liquidity.

## 4. Behavioral Signals

### NLP Models
- **Sentiment analysis**: Gauge market sentiment from text data.
- **Event detection**: Identify significant events from news and social media.

### Volume Spikes
- **Volume analysis**: Identify significant volume changes.
- **Liquidity patterns**: Monitor market depth and liquidity.

## 5. Execution Logic

### Dynamic Position Sizing
- **Risk management**: Adjust position sizes based on risk tolerance.
- **Liquidity awareness**: Ensure trades are executed efficiently.

### Exit Strategies
- **Trailing stops**: Adjust stop-loss levels based on market movements.
- **Time-based exits**: Exit positions after a set time period.

## 6. Socratic Questions

- What narrative is driving this price action?
- Why is volume spiking here?
- What does the order book dynamics reveal about market intent?
- How does sentiment analysis indicate potential regime shifts?

## Constraints
- **Access to real-time data feeds and NLP tools**: Ensure reliable data sources.
- **Focus on capturing short-term momentum while managing drawdowns**: Balance risk and reward.
